Organic Sage Services

Agriculture is, at its core, man’s management and influence over living systems. This management requires balancing a complex and ever changing set of variables. Organic Sage Consulting offers the insight of a life spent working in agriculture to help you build this balance. We bring a commitment to farming and its integral connection to life and the environment. If you want to build sustainable value in your living system then Organic Sage Consulting wants to help you. Organic Sage offers consulting services in:

  • Farm development planning, old, and new
  • Animal husbandry: dairy, meat (beef, swine, goat, sheep, equine, exotic) draft, companion
  • Poultry management: layers, broilers, turkeys, geese, guinea fowl, ducks
  • Farm design: (fields, structures, operations) and biodiversity
  • Farm operations and systems implementation
  • Market identification and development
  • Product development: (live, processed, single ingredient, multiple ingredient, dried, frozen, fresh, milled, cooked, hot sealed, liquid and more)
  • Food safety and HACCP plan development
  • Farm safety
  • Succession planning
  • Organic to USDA NOP all scopes
  • Non-GMO Project and/or EU NonGMO all scopes
  • Humane Farm Animal Care-Certified Humane all eligible species
  • Global Organic Textiles
  • Social Accountability International
  • HACCP
  • Best Management Practices
  • LEAN approach tailored to your operations
